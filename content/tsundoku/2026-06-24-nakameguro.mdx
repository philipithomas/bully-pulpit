This is my favorite area of Tokyo, so I decided to stay nearby this trip. In a moment of jet lag, I found that the Starbucks Reserve was the only cafe open early: at 7:00. While waiting for it to open, I found this beautiful bridge outside. A woman saw me taking photos and suggested photographing a historic bridge down the canal. It was not until writing this post that I realized she was not referring to this pink bridge, but instead the one next to it.
