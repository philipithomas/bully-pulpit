I stumbled upon this shrine while wandering in Kyoto. It commemorates [a story involving boars](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Go%C5%8D_Shrine), and the grounds are full of boar statues and figurines. The figurines contain fortunes, and people write wishes on the outside before leaving them around the shrine. I do not know why, but I found it fascinating.
