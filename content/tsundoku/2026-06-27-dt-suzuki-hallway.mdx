I walked past a [castle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kanazawa_Castle) on my way to this museum and thought, "Is it worth prioritizing this museum over a castle?" I can confidently say it was worth it.

The [D. T. Suzuki Museum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/D._T._Suzuki_Museum) commemorates the life of Buddhist philosopher [D. T. Suzuki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/D._T._Suzuki), who [traveled the world teaching](https://www.newyorker.com/magazine/1957/08/31/great-simplicity). The museum is beautifully designed as a place for contemplation and reflection.
