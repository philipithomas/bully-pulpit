I have read a few of Haruki Murakami's books, both fiction and non-fiction, so I stopped by his eponymous library at Waseda University. I enjoyed walking around and seeing remnants of his old jazz bar, then spent an hour in the listening lounge, where they play records from his vinyl collection.
