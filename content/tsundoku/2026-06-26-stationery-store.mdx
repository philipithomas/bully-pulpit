I went out to Karuizawa to have lunch with [Luis](https://www.luismendo.com/) and [Yuka](https://www.yukamendo.com/). I had never been, and the town surprised me. I had heard that it is known as the "Hamptons of Tokyo," but it felt more like Boulder, Colorado: up in the mountains, with a mix of nature, a clear city center, and even a Patagonia store.

While walking around, I found this old stationery store.
