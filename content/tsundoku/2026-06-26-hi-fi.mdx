Jazz kissaten pride themselves on hi-fi listening setups. [Bokunen's website](http://www.ebokunen.com/) lists the exact equipment they use. Visitors face the speakers, with the best seats in the middle. The sound quality is sublime. It is a bar built for listening, not talking.
