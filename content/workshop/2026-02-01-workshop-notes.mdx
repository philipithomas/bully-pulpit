Welcome to the Workshop. This is where I share notes on work in progress — the messy middle of building things.

## Current projects

I'm rebuilding my personal site from a Ghost blog to a Next.js application. The goal is to consolidate philipithomas.com and contraption.co into a single site.

### Why migrate?

Ghost is excellent blogging software, but I want more control over the reading experience and deeper integration between the site and my email infrastructure.

## Tooling

- **Next.js** for the frontend, with MDX for content
- **Rust** for the email backend (printing-press)
- **PostgreSQL** for subscriber data
- **AWS SES** for email delivery

More notes to come as the project progresses.
