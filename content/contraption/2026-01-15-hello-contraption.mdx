This is the first post in the Contraption newsletter — essays and launches about building things.

## Why write publicly?

Writing forces clarity of thought. When you commit ideas to paper, you discover what you actually believe versus what you merely assume.

There's something powerful about thinking out loud. It invites collaboration, surfaces blind spots, and creates a record of how ideas evolve over time.

## What to expect

I'll write here about product design, engineering decisions, and the occasional launch announcement. Some posts will be technical, others philosophical. All will be honest about what I'm learning.
