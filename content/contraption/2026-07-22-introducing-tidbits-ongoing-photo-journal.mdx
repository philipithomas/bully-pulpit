During my trip to Japan, I published the pop-up photography newsletter [*Tsundoku*](/introducing-tsundoku). It gave me an excuse to wake up at 4 a.m. to catch the sunrise light, then forced me to look closely at my photos and choose the handful to edit and publish.

Back in San Francisco, I have gotten involved in a photography group that gives me a reason to keep taking pictures. I have been [off social media for years](/how-to-replace-social-media-with-a-personal-newsletter), so I have had fun turning this site into a place to share them.

Now that *Tsundoku* has ended, I am starting an ongoing photography newsletter called *tidbits*. I still think there is something powerful about giving a newsletter a start and a stop. With photography, that structure lets the photographs tell a cohesive story. I may create more pop-up newsletters for future trips or events. *tidbits* is for everything in between: an ongoing place to share the photographs I take.
