Today I'm publishing an conversation I had with [Aaron Cohn](https://aaroncohn.com) for the [Contraption Company podcast](https://podcast.contraption.co). Aaron is the co-founder of [realnice](https://getrealnice.com), a simple personal website builder. realnice competes with my product, [Postcard](https://postcard.page), which is also a personal website builder. So, I thought it would be fun to sit down with a competitor and talk shop.

This isn't the first time that Aaron and I have competed. In this episode, we discuss how we met, our different approaches to building website software, no-code, craft versus indie software, and more. This conversation offers a deep dive into the nuances of modern software development, valuable for anyone interested in the tech industry's latest trends.
