## Data collection

This site collects minimal data:

- **Email address**: When you subscribe to a newsletter.
- **Name**: Only if voluntarily provided.
- **Email delivery data**: Which emails were sent and delivery errors.

## Analytics

This site uses Vercel Web Analytics and Speed Insights to measure traffic and performance in aggregate. These tools are cookieless and do not track you across sites.

## What we do not track

- No open or click tracking in email.
- No advertising or cross-site tracking.

## Cookies

A single authentication cookie (`bp_token`) is set when you sign in. It is used solely to identify your session. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

## Email

All emails are sent from mail@philipithomas.com via AWS SES. Each email includes an unsubscribe link. We do not sell or share your email address.

## Data retention

Your email address is stored as long as you remain subscribed. Upon unsubscribing, your data is retained for record-keeping but no further emails are sent.

## Third-party services

- **Vercel**: Vercel hosts and serves this site, including Vercel Web Analytics and Speed Insights.
- **AI model providers**: AI features route through the Vercel AI Gateway to third-party model providers, currently Anthropic for the Bell assistant and OpenAI for search embeddings. These providers process the messages you send to Bell in order to generate responses. Under the terms of their APIs, they do not use this content to train models.
- **AWS SES**: Amazon Simple Email Service delivers all email.
- **Neon**: Neon hosts the subscriber database.
- **Chroma**: Chroma Cloud hosts the site search index and processes search queries.
