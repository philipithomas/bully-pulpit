## Typographical

Three typefaces from [Klim Type Foundry](https://klim.co.nz/) in Wellington, New Zealand.

**Söhne** is the sans-serif for headings, navigation, and interface elements. Book and semibold weights, with italics for both.

**Tiempos Text** is the serif for body copy and long-form prose. Book weight with italics.

**Söhne Mono** is the monospace for code blocks, dates, and metadata. Book and semibold weights.


## Technical

The [site](https://github.com/philipithomas/bully-pulpit) is a Next.js application with MDX content, statically generated at build time and hosted on [Vercel](https://vercel.com/). Search uses [Chroma](https://www.trychroma.com/) with SPLADE embeddings.

A Mac Mini at my home in San Francisco handles email delivery, analytics, and a handful of other services through Docker containers behind a Cloudflare Tunnel. I wrote about the motivation in [A mini data center](/a-mini-data-center) and published a complete setup guide in [How to host web apps on a Mac Mini](/how-to-host-web-apps-on-a-mac-mini). The framework powering it, [Toolbox](https://github.com/contraptionco/toolbox), is open source.

Email delivery runs through [Printing Press](https://github.com/philipithomas/printing-press) on the Mac Mini. Analytics run through a self-hosted [Plausible](https://plausible.io/) instance on the same machine. No cookies, no tracking scripts from third parties, no personal data collected.

## Style guide

Rules I try to follow when writing:

- Active voice
- No contractions
- No em dashes (use other punctuation instead)
- Never use the word "very"
- Avoid exclamation points
- Oxford commas (always use the serial comma)
- Sentence case for titles
- Do not use "+" in place of "and"
- No redundant questions
- Dates in ISO-8601 format
- Units in metric, temperatures in Celsius
- Assume international readers (country codes on phone numbers, country in addresses)
- Never use the structure "it is not (this), it is (that)"
