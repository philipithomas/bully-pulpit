I enjoy fine dining as a lens to explore local culture and craftspeople performing at the highest level.

## How I count

I count each restaurant once, no matter how often I return. Michelin stars reflect the rating in the [Michelin Guide](https://guide.michelin.com/en/restaurants) at the time I visited, with some grace for nearby promotions and places the Guide reached later. World rankings reflect the list in the [World’s 50 Best archives](https://www.theworlds50best.com/restaurants/best-in-the-world/previous-list/) at the time I visited.
