## ✨ What I've been up to

April was a blur - I was in the Bay Area, NYC, and Cleveland. I'm looking forward to spending the next three weeks without travel.   
  
I am currently immersed in the development of [Booklet](https://booklet.community), a new product from [Contraption Co.](https://contraption.co) designed for online online communities. I started building Booklet to address the shortcomings of using real-time chat apps for communities. Booklet strives to be calmer - it combines a forum and member directory, and generates a customized email newsletter for the [99%](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1%25_rule) of community members who consume content rather than create it. If you're intrigued and want early access, register at [booklet.community](https://booklet.community).
