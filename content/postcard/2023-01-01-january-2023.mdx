✨ **Highlights of last month**

- Traveled to Hilton Head, Savannah, and Denver for the holidays
- Had a couple of flights canceled by Southwest
- Published "[Why I built Postcard: A calmer alternative to social networks](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/why-i-built-postcard-a-calmer-alternative-to-social-networks)" and "[How to replace social media with a personal newsletter](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/how-to-replace-social-media-with-a-personal-newsletter)", which were popular online 

**🤔 Things and thoughts to share**

- Articles I've been reading: [‘Luddite’ Teens Don’t Want Your Likes](https://www.nytimes.com/2022/12/15/style/teens-social-media.html), [How I got my attention back](https://craigmod.com/essays/how_i_got_my_attention_back/), [Overton window (Wikipedia)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Overton_window)
- Been reading [Novelist as a Vocation](https://www.amazon.com/Novelist-as-Vocation-Haruki-Murakami-ebook/dp/B09S37T6TM/ref=tmm_kin_swatch_0?_encoding=UTF8&qid=1673027309&sr=8-2) by Haruki Murakami. Lots of great insights - including how he avoids writer's block. 
- Been drinking the [Prolog Jorge Vasquez Geisha](https://www.prologcoffee.com/collections/buy-our-coffee/products/roble-negro-washed-geisha-1?variant=39793244405825), which is one of the better coffees I've had. Also, I've been enjoying a Kenyan coffee from [Kahiwa Coffee Roasters](https://www.kahiwacoffee.fi/) in Finland. Kahiwa caught my attention because it's co-owned by F1 driver Valtteri Bottas.  
- [PromptHunt](https://prompthunt.com) aggregates the prompts that people are using with AI. "Prompt developer" is an emerging job.  
- Apple's new [end-to-end iCloud encryption](https://techcrunch.com/2022/12/07/apple-launches-end-to-end-encryption-for-icloud-data/?guccounter=1) is a win for consumer privacy - I'm in the process of switching from Google Photos to iCloud because of this.
- Why are we rating everything? A former #1 restaurant in the world only has 4.6 stars on Google. Is that really useful?
