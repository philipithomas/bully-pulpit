Here's my monthly newsletter about what I'm up to, which [I send in place of social media](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/how-to-replace-social-media-with-a-personal-newsletter).

**What I did in February**

- Finished my residence at [Almost Perfect](https://www.almostperfect.jp/) in Tokyo. I gave a presentation _Rethinking Work Beyond the Factory Model_, about how most of our knowledge work practices have carried over from factories, and are in the process of being rethought. The original talk wasn't recorded, but I [published a write-up and recording on the Contraption Company website](https://www.contraption.co/essays/rethinking-work-beyond-the-factory/). 
- I was fascinated by [stamp culture in Japan](https://hatsukoi.co.uk/blog/108-the-art-and-tradition-of-japanese-hanko.html#:~:text=Deeply%20rooted%20in%20Japanese%20history,place%20of%20a%20written%20signature.). When I returned home, I made some stamps so that I could stamp my own business cards. I've been collecting different business card papers. 
- I enjoyed some amazing coffees in Japan. [Leaves Coffee](https://leavescoffee.jp/en) and [Glitch Coffee](https://shop.glitchcoffee.com/en) were good enough to be among "my top cafes in the world" (alongside [April](https://www.aprilcoffeeroasters.com/) in Copenhagen, [Tim Wendelboe](https://www.contraption.co/essays/indie-to-micro/) in Oslo, and [Substance Cafe](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/slow-travel-in-paris-discovering-substance-cafe) in Paris). 
- I'm experimenting with more content from Contraption Company, and published an [podcast-style interview I did with Aaron Cohn](https://www.contraption.co/essays/talking-shop-aaron-cohn-realnice/) about our competing website builders. We chatted about personal email newsletters, craft software versus indie companies, and web trends. If you want to follow future conversations I have,[subscribe at podcast.contraption.co](https://podcast.contraption.co).
- [Booklet](https://booklet.group) had a lot of improvements - including [push notifications and PWA support](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/introducing-push-notifications-and-pwa-support), [unified login](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/one-place-to-sign-in-to-all-booklet-communities), [search](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/introducing-search-on-booklet-communities), [passwordless login](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/passwordless-login-changes), [mentions](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/you-can-now-mention-people-on-booklet), and [a refreshed website](https://booklet.group).  I'm embarking on some ambitious new features this month that will drastically improve the product. 
- I was a [guest on the SaaS Growth Podcast](https://carlanderson.xyz/saas-growth/episode-8/), where I talked about running multiple products, building a product studio, asynchronous communication, and building differentiated products.
