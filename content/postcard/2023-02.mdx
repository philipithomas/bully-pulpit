**✨ What I've been up to**  
  
Hello from Cozumel, Mexico! It's 80° F here - a welcome break from the Chicago Winter.   
  
Since last Summer, I've been taking a "solo week" trip every quarter as a creative recharge. This trip to Cozumel is one of these solo weeks, and I've been spending a lot of time in cafes exploring new [Contraption Company](https://contraption.co) projects. It's a ritual I plan to continue - I've already scheduled solo weeks over the next year to Copenhagen, Mexico City, And Tokyo.  
  
Postcard continues to do well. I [built new features](https://updates.postcard.page/posts/new-on-postcard-featured-posts-email-list-imports-and-more) and did a [short interview with GoSolo](https://gosolo.subkit.com/postcard).    
  
[Robin Sloan mentioned Postcard in his newsletter](https://www.robinsloan.com/lab/attention-router/). I enjoyed Robin's book [Mr. Penumbra's 24-Hour Bookstore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mr._Penumbra%27s_24-Hour_Bookstore) while living in the San Francisco neighborhood where the story is set.
