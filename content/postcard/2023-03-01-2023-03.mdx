**✨ What I've been up to**  
  
Part of my work at [The Contraption Company](https://contraption.co) includes helping startups develop new products. For the last six months, I've unintentionally been working almost exclusively with AI-focused startups, and I've seen the rise of OpenAI first-hand.   
  
While the broader tech market shows signs of slowing, the hype in AI has been exploding. One Contraption Co. client went from an idea to \>$7m in seed funding within five weeks. (They're still in stealth - but are hiring in engineering, growth, and design. Email me for an intro.)   
  
I summarized my observations and predictions about this AI hype cycle in this post: [OpenAI, the path for OpenAI-powered startups, and the AI hype cycle](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/openai-the-path-for-openai-powered-startups-and-the-ai-hype-cycle).  
  
Finally, I've been contemplating this thought: Businesses like to buy from businesses, and people like to buy from people.
