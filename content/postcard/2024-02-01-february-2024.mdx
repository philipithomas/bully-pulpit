Here's my monthly newsletter about what I'm up to, which [I send in place of social media](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/how-to-replace-social-media-with-a-personal-newsletter).

## What I did in January

- Hello from Tokyo! I'm here doing a creative residence at [Almost Perfect](https://almostperfect.jp/). If you're in town, come join my presentation [Rethinking Work Beyond the Factory Model](https://www.instagram.com/almostperfecttokyo/p/C2ye8uRStpU/) tomorrow night. 
- I published [Digital Product Studios](https://www.contraption.co/essays/digital-product-studios/), an essay about product studios and a return to craft-focused knowledge work. It includes some photos of my trip to a watchmaking studio in Morioka.  
- [FRCTNL](https://frctnl.xyz), the community for fractional tech workers I run, has steadily been growing. Come check it out if you're interested in part-time technology work. 
- I've been hosting 6-person dinners for people in the tech industry in New York. If you're interested in joining a future one, let me know.
- I've been building a lot of new things on [Booklet](https://booklet.group): [More design refreshes](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/updated-layout-for-posts-and-some-other-booklet-im), [a powerful search engine leveraging OpenAI](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/introducing-search-on-booklet-communities), and [image improvements](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/image-improvements-on-booklet) - with more coming soon. It seems to be working - usage and revenue are steadily increasing.
