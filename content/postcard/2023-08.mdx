I'm [Philip](https://www.philipithomas.com), and this is my monthly newsletter about what I'm up to, which [I send in place of social media](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/how-to-replace-social-media-with-a-personal-newsletter).   
  
**✨ What I was up to in July**  
  
[Booklet](https://booklet.community) is a product I've been building to help communities communicate in a more structured and readable way. This month I finally had some people besides me start to use it. It's been fun to get the product into the wild, and now I'm iterating rapidly based on their feedback ahead of a more public release.   
  
I finally got to reading the [Noma Guide to Fermentation](https://bookshop.org/p/books/the-noma-guide-to-fermentation-including-koji-kombuchas-shoyus-misos-vinegars-garums-lacto-ferments-and-black-fruits-and-vegetables-david-zilber/6900391) after visiting there last summer, and I've been doing a lot of experiments with lacto-fermentation. (See some photos below). It's been fun to go to the farmer's market every week and to cook with what's local and seasonal.  
   
I recently celebrated one full year of working full-time on [Contraption Company](https://contraption.co). Building new ways to help people work online has been a fun, rewarding, and creatively fulfilling journey. I'm proud of our commitment to keeping the business intentionally small and independent, and I'm excited about what the next year holds.
