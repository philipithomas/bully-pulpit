This is my monthly newsletter about what I'm up to, which [I send in place of social media](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/how-to-replace-social-media-with-a-personal-newsletter).

## What I did in November

- Started writing some essays about online work, dependability, tools, and craft on the Contraption Company site. [Subscribe there](https://www.contraption.co/essays/) if you want to follow them. First essays: [Booklet's Architecture](https://www.contraption.co/essays/booklet-architecture/), [One Year of Dependable Software](https://www.contraption.co/essays/one-year-of-dependable-software/), and [The Transition from Indiehacking to Micro Companies](https://www.contraption.co/essays/indie-to-micro/).
- Had a great food-focused trip through Europe with a friend. I started up north in Oslo, and ended south in Barcelona. I went to some old favorites such as [Noma](https://noma.dk/), [April](https://www.aprilcoffeeroasters.com/), [Popl](https://poplburger.com/), and [Dishoom](https://www.dishoom.com/), and found some new favorites such as [Disfrutar](https://www.disfrutarbarcelona.com/?lang=en) and [Silo](https://www.silolondon.com/) (whose[book I read in April](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/what-i-m-up-to-april-2023)). Highlights included jumping in the Oslofjord from a floating sauna, eating a food that's legal in only three countries, walking an F1 track, and seeing the [Pope's old residence](https://philips-photography.postcard.page/posts/palais-des-papes). I also got to meet customers of Contraption Company products along the way.
- Started a [Postcard for some photography experiments](https://philips-photography.postcard.page/posts) - [here's my favorite](https://philips-photography.postcard.page/posts/ostmarka) so far. 
- Continuing to work hard on [Booklet](https://booklet.group). A lot of new communities and members joined in November, and I'm working hard on some new features.
