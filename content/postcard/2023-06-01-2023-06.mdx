## ✨ What I've been up to

It's been a busy month at [Contraption Company](https://www.contraption.co/). I'm amid building a new product called [Booklet](https://booklet.community), which I plan to launch this Summer. It's a community platform designed to be a calmer alternative to Discord and Slack. I've also been helping some startups launch new generative AI products, which has been rewarding.  
  
My 2015 article [How Operations Research and Artificial Intelligence Overlap](https://blog.staffjoy.com/how-operations-research-and-artificial-intelligence-overlap-b128a3efee2e) has been picking up traction as AI is in the zeitgeist.  
  
I've been trying to use my Airpods less and be bored more often. It's so easy to plug in and tune out the world.   
  
I got a shout-out in [Emma's Indiehackers podcast](https://share.transistor.fm/s/21456c85).
