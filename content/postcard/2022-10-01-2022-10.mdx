**🤔 Things worth sharing**

- I started replacing work-related video calls with "walk and talk" phone calls. It's also been a fun way to talk to friends. If you want to chat, [let's walk and talk](https://calendly.com/philipithomas/walk-and-talk?month=2022-10). 
- **Books:** [Small Giants](https://amzn.to/3ClGPsT) is a good reminder of small businesses' impacts on local communities. [Status and Culture](https://amzn.to/3CncvPz) made me realize that many online communities function as scoreboards for niche status groups. For example, [Strava](https://www.strava.com/) measures how quickly bicyclists ride common routes, and [Nomad List](https://nomadlist.com/) measures how many places travelers have visited. 
- **Startups:** [Nexus](https://nexus.site/) combines checking + investment accounts into a single, auto-managed tool that keeps cash consistently invested but instantly accessible. [Kindred](https://livekindred.com/) lets you swap homes - we've used it while traveling a few times already, and it's an almost-free alternative to Airbnb.
- **Gadgets:** [Adhesive pen loops](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002CWMAPW/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o08_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1) are a helpful add-on for any notebook.
