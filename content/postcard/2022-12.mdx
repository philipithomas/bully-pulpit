✨ **Highlights of last month**

- [Launched Postcard](https://www.contraption.co/news/launching-postcard). Had \>15k visitors on the first day and hundreds of signups, which was more than I expected. Overall, a great start!
- Did a [smaller launch for The Contraption Company](https://www.contraption.co/news/introducing-contraption-co), the product studio I started where I'm building tools like Postcard.
- Spent a week in London and took a spontaneous side trip to Copenhagen. I enjoyed lots of fantastic coffee - highlights included [Prolog](https://www.prologcoffee.com/), [April](https://www.aprilcoffeeroasters.com/), [Prufrock](https://prufrockcoffee.com/), [Origin](https://www.origincoffee.co.uk/pages/london), and [Omotesando](https://omotesandokoffeecafe.com/).  And, the new King waved at (or toward) me 👋
- I joined as a guest caller on one of my favorite podcasts, [Cal Newport's Deep Questions](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0QrVkmwLGCA&t=2080s).
- Moonlight, the company I co-founded in 2017 and sold in 2020, was [just purchased by its top customer](https://www.moonlightwork.com/blog/moonlight-new-owners). I'm happy to see it continue to exist, and I've been helping the new team.
