My monthly newsletter about what I am up to, which [I send in place of social media](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/how-to-replace-social-media-with-a-personal-newsletter).

## What I did in June

- I left [Chroma](/chroma), and I start a new role in August.
- In this liminal time, I am spending a couple of weeks in Japan and practicing photography. I started a pop-up newsletter called [*Tsundoku*](/tsundoku) sharing photos from the trip. Take a look.
- I published [Spring cleaning](/spring-cleaning) about pruning old projects.
- I spent a lot of time listening to music. I saw James Blake perform on his Trying Times tour, went to two baroque performances in Berkeley, and have been hopping between hi-fi jazz kissaten in Japan. A high point was stumbling into [Lion](https://maps.app.goo.gl/QA6XCsyAgJgcn9rR7?g_st=ic), where they happened to be playing two of my favorite pieces: Dvorak's cello concerto and Kabalevsky's cello concerto.
- I had a straight flush in a poker game.
