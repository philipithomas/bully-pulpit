## What I did in December

- I'm amid some big life changes. I'm joining an AI startup in San Francisco, and moving out of NYC. I wrote more here: [**Lost and found**](https://www.contraption.co/lost-and-found/).
- Visited [Prodigal Coffee](https://getprodigal.com/)'s roastery in Colorado, which was fun. I've been mail-ordering their beans and it was fun to get a behind-the-scenes peek.
- Spent some time in Boulder and loved the [Portal pop-up sauna](https://www.portalthermaculture.com/). I wish there were more social saunas in the USA, especially in San Francisco.
- Made the decision to move out of New York City, and had vacated it within two weeks.
