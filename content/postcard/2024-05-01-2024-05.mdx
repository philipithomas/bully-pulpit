**What I did in April**

- Cat's out of the bag - the "Shenanigans" I referenced in [March](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/what-i-m-up-to-march-2024) and [April](https://www.philipithomas.com/posts/what-i-m-up-to-april-2024) are revealed. I eloped and got married in a small village in Mexico about six weeks ago. Text or email me for photos and more details. 
- Took some French lessons at the FIAF in NYC. It was a fun experiment, but I unfortunately don't think I have the time or headspace to continue them. Pronunciation was more subtle than I expected. 
- I've been leaning into more intentionally analog habits, with [single-purpose notebooks](https://www.thedeeplife.com/podcasts/episodes/ep-292-single-purpose-notebooks/) a at the core. Instead of doing most of my daily writing + notes in Day One or Obsidian, I'm mostly just carrying a stack of [these Leuchtturm1927 notebooks](https://geni.us/philips-notebooks).

- [Rewrote Booklet's docs](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/better-docs) and [overhauled its member management tools](https://hq.booklet.group/posts/better-member-managment). 
- Started a three-month experiment in eating a plant-based diet. It's been surprisingly easy and enjoyable. I've been eating a lot of [Bottas-inspired porridge](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NkQcC12acm4), [Rancho Gordo beans](https://www.ranchogordo.com/), rices, lacto-fermented vegetables (I had made a lot of fermented daikon), and [Noma's vegan xo sauce](https://nomaprojects.com/products/vegan-xo-sauce) and [other projects](https://nomaprojects.com/products/ocean-bundle).
